## Executive Summary

NovaCRM delivered $473,400 in revenue for February 2026, $15,400 (3.36%) above budget, driven primarily by SaaS subscription outperformance and accelerating usage-based revenue. Total OpEx of $352,400 ran $9,900 (2.89%) over budget, with contractor spend, legal fees, and recruiting costs partially offsetting favorable variances in engineering salaries and paid advertising. Operating income of $31,300 exceeded budget by $1,300 (4.33%), though gross margin came in at 81.05% versus the budgeted 81.33% due to elevated third-party API costs.

**3 items require leadership attention:**

Combined with the $6,000 favorable variance in Engineering Salaries, this pattern suggests headcount gaps are being filled with contractors at a higher blended cost; the offset is not dollar-for-dollar and the contractor run rate needs to be assessed before it compounds further.
2. Legal & Professional Fees (6310) reached $11,200 against a $6,000 budget, a $5,200 (86.67%) unfavorable variance and nearly double the prior period spend of $5,800. The nature of this engagement is not specified in the data [VERIFY: confirm whether this relates to financing activity, customer contracts, employment matters, or other legal work to assess recurrence and expected duration].
3. Professional Services Revenue (4200) missed budget by $6,500 (14.44%) and declined $2,700 month-over-month. Two consecutive months of decline against a $45,000 budget warrant a pipeline review to determine whether this reflects timing of project starts or a structural shortfall in services bookings.
