FAQ Frequently asked questions How does the pilot work? The Pilot is a fixed-term 60–90 day proof engagement at $1,500/month, designed to prove the workflow on your real data before you commit to a recurring subscription. Scope is intentionally constrained: 1 memo per month, 72-hour SLA, 1 data source, read-only portal access. You also get weekly feedback calls so we calibrate the memo to your board’s voice and refine your COA mapping together. At the end of the term, you either move to Starter (or Pro) for the recurring workflow, or stop cleanly with no further obligation. What happens after the pilot? Most pilots convert to Starter ($3–4K/month), which is the recurring monthly variance memo workflow with full team portal access, audit log, and version history. If your team wants more deliverables — KPI snapshots, prior-period trend analysis, cash and forecast commentary, board-ready monthly summary, quarterly business review — that is what Pro ($6–8K/month) adds. Pro is not gated on multi-entity; it is for the same single-entity team that wants richer finance output. Multi-entity is a separate axis, available as an add-on (see below). Can I try before committing? Yes. We offer a free sample memo generated from your last month’s GL export. Send us the data, and we will produce a board-ready variance memo so you can evaluate quality, traceability, and format before making any commitment. Book an evaluation below to get started. What’s your cancellation policy? After the Pilot, all plans are month-to-month with 30-day written notice to cancel. No long-term contracts required. We retain your data per your retention policy and provide a full data export on request upon cancellation. Do you offer annual pricing? Yes. Annual commitments receive a discount on all tiers. Contact us during your evaluation to discuss annual pricing options tailored to your team’s needs and reporting cadence. What about multi-entity or multiple accounting systems? Both are available as stackable add-ons on Starter and Pro. Additional entities are priced at $1,500 per entity per month — you stay on your current tier and only pay for the complexity you actually have. Additional accounting systems are scoped by quote (most teams need this only when consolidating across QBO + NetSuite or similar). If you have 4 or more entities, multiple accounting systems, custom retention or legal-hold requirements, or need VPC deployment, Enterprise is the better fit. We will scope which path makes sense during your evaluation call.
