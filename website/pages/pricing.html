Pricing Factors

What drives price

Your price depends on five factors. Every proposal is scoped transparently.

Data Sources

QBO, Xero, NetSuite, or custom GL exports. More sources increase integration scope.

Entities

Single entity or multi-entity consolidation. Each entity adds complexity to variance analysis.

SLA Tier

Standard 48-hour, 24-hour, or 4-hour close-week turnaround on generated memos.

Support Level

Email support, dedicated reviewer, priority channel, or dedicated reviewer pool.

Custom Features

Custom COA templates, retention policies, VPC deployment, or bespoke integrations.
