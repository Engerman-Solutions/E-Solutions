Pricing Factors

What drives price

Your price depends on five factors. Every proposal is scoped transparently.

Data Sources

QBO, Xero, NetSuite, or custom GL exports. More sources increase integration scope.

Entities

Single entity or multi-entity consolidation. Each entity adds complexity to variance analysis.

SLA Tier

Standard 72-hour (Pilot), 48-hour (Starter), 24-hour (Pro), or 4-hour close-week turnaround on generated memos.

Support Level

Email support, dedicated reviewer, priority channel, or dedicated reviewer pool.

Custom Features

Custom COA templates, retention policies, VPC deployment, or bespoke integrations.

The Pilot is a fixed-term 60–90 day proof engagement at $1,500/month, designed to prove the workflow on your real data before you commit to a recurring subscription. Scope is intentionally constrained: 1 memo per month, 72-hour SLA, 1 data source, read-only portal access. You also get weekly feedback calls so we calibrate the memo to your board’s voice and refine your COA mapping together. At the end of the term, you either move to Starter (or Pro) for the recurring workflow, or stop cleanly with no further obligation.

Most pilots convert to Starter ($3–4K/month), which is the recurring monthly variance memo workflow with full team portal access, audit log, and version history.
