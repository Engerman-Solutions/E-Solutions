## Navigation Labels (High Information Scent)

| Label | Destination | Scent Rationale |
|-------|------------|-----------------|
| **How It Works** | `/#how-it-works` | Controller/FP&A question: "How does it connect to my data?" |
| **Sample Output** | `/sample-output` | CFO question: "What will I actually get?" |
| **Pricing** | `/pricing` | Budget check: "Is this in my range?" |
| **Security** | `/security` | Procurement: "Is this safe and governed?" |
| **Customers** | `/customers` | Social proof: "Who else uses this?" |
| **Book Evaluation** | `/pricing#evaluation` | High-intent CTA |

Labels are literal, not clever. They match what finance buyers search for during evaluation.
