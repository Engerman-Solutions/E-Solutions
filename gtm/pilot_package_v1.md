# E-Solutions Design Partner Pilot

**Board-ready variance memos in hours — with reproducible numbers and an audit trail.**

---

## Who this is for

Seed to Series B SaaS and services companies with lean finance teams (1–3 people) that produce monthly board or investor reporting packages. You use QuickBooks, Xero, or NetSuite. Your controller spends 8–15 hours every month manually assembling variance memos from spreadsheets.

## What you get

Each month, E-Solutions delivers a board-ready variance memo produced from your actual financial data:

- **Validated data intake** — we ingest your GL export and budget file, validate against your chart of accounts, and flag any data issues before analysis begins
- **Variance analysis** — automated comparison of actuals vs. budget and prior period across all account categories, with variance calculations and materiality flagging
- **Narrative explanations** — AI-generated draft narratives explaining the key drivers behind each material variance, written in board-appropriate language
- **KPI summary** — topline performance metrics, gross margin, headcount, and custom KPIs relevant to your business
- **Human QA review** — every memo is reviewed by a finance QA reviewer before delivery. No memo reaches your inbox without human verification.
- **Audit trail** — every step from data upload to final delivery is checkpointed and traceable. You can reproduce any memo from its source data at any time.
- **Secure delivery** — final memo delivered via encrypted portal download and email

**Turnaround:** Under 48 hours from validated data upload (Starter SLA).

## What you provide

- **Monthly GL export** (CSV or Excel) from your accounting system
- **Budget file** (CSV or Excel) — your board-approved annual budget broken down by month

## How the workflow works

1. **Upload** — your controller uploads the GL export and budget file via our secure portal
2. **Validate** — we validate the data against your chart of accounts, run schema checks, and flag any issues. Nothing proceeds to analysis until validation passes.
3. **Analyze** — our variance engine computes variances, identifies material items, and generates draft narratives
4. **Review** — a human finance QA reviewer checks the draft for accuracy, clarity, and completeness
5. **Approve** — you review the memo in the portal and approve for final delivery (or request revisions)
6. **Deliver** — board-ready memo delivered as PDF via portal download and encrypted email

- **You see a sample memo before committing.** We'll produce one using anonymized data so you can evaluate quality and format before providing real financials.

- **No long-term commitment.** The pilot is 90 days. If it doesn't save time and improve quality, you walk away.
