# Outreach Operating Rhythm v1

**Purpose:** Define the weekly cadence, tracking metrics, and feedback loops for founder-led outbound.

---

## Weekly Cadence

| Day | Activity | Time Budget |
|-----|----------|-------------|
| Monday | Score and prioritize new prospects. Update target list. | 1 hour |
| Tuesday–Thursday | Send outreach (emails + LinkedIn DMs). Follow up on open threads. | 30 min/day |
| Friday | Log results. Review reply rates. Update objection notes. | 30 min |

**Total weekly time commitment:** ~3.5 hours

---

## Weekly Targets

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| New prospects scored | 5–8 | Use account targeting rubric |
| First-touch messages sent | 8–12 | Mix of email and LinkedIn |
| Follow-ups sent | 5–8 | Day 4–5 and Day 10–12 variants |
| Breakup messages sent | 2–3 | Day 20–25 for non-responders |
| Calls scheduled | 1–2 | From prior week's replies |
| Calls completed | 1–2 | Follow design partner call flow |

These are targets for a solo founder doing outreach part-time. Adjust based on pipeline health.

---

## Reply Tracking

### Reply Categories

| Category | Definition | Action |
|----------|------------|--------|
| **Positive** | Interested, wants to see more, asks questions | Send sample memo + pilot package within 1 hour. Schedule call. |
| **Warm** | Acknowledges but defers ("not now", "maybe next quarter") | Add to nurture list. Follow up in 4–6 weeks. |
| **Negative** | Explicit no or not interested | Log reason. Remove from active list. |
| **Referral** | Not a fit but offers an intro | Thank them. Follow up on the referral within 24 hours. |
| **No reply** | No response after full sequence (3 touches + breakup) | Mark as exhausted. Revisit in 3 months if pipeline is thin. |

### Reply Rate Benchmarks

For founder-led cold outreach to ICP-fit prospects:

| Channel | Expected Reply Rate | Good | Concerning |
|---------|-------------------|------|------------|
| Email (cold) | 8–15% | >15% | <5% |
| LinkedIn DM | 10–20% | >20% | <8% |
| Email (warm intro) | 30–50% | >50% | <25% |

If reply rates are below "concerning" for 2+ weeks, revisit messaging and targeting.

## No-Reply Handling

### Outreach Sequence

| Touch | Timing | Message |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Day 0 | First-touch email or LinkedIn DM |
| 2 | Day 4–5 | Follow-up 1 (attach sample memo) |
| 3 | Day 10–12 | Follow-up 2 (value add, offer free test run) |
| 4 | Day 20–25 | Breakup message |

After the breakup message, stop. Do not send a 5th touch. Mark as "sequence complete" in the target list.

### When to Restart a Sequence

Restart outreach only if one of these triggers occurs:
- Company raises a new round
- New Controller or CFO hire announced
- Prospect engages with content (likes a post, views LinkedIn profile)
- 3+ months have passed since last touch

---

## Feedback Logging

After every call or meaningful reply, log the following in the target list:

1. **Interest level:** Hot / Warm / Cold / Disqualified
2. **Objections raised:** Use the categories from the objection handling table
3. **Pricing reaction:** Positive / Neutral / Pushback / Did not discuss
4. **Feature requests:** Anything they asked for that we do not have
5. **Competitive mentions:** Any tools or services they compared us to
6. **Quotable feedback:** Direct quotes worth capturing for positioning refinement

### Weekly Review Questions

At the end of each week, answer these:

1. What objection came up most? Do we need a better response?
2. Are we reaching the right person? (CFO vs. Controller vs. Founder)
3. Which message variant got the most replies?
4. Did any prospect describe the problem differently than we expected?
5. Should we adjust the ICP based on what we learned?

---

## Monthly Review

At the end of each month, review the full pipeline:

| Question | What to Look At |
|----------|----------------|
| Are we generating enough conversations? | Total calls scheduled vs. target (4–8/month) |
| Are conversations converting? | Calls → test runs → pilots |
| Is the ICP holding? | Are strong-fit prospects actually converting better? |
| Is pricing landing? | How many cite $1,500/month as a blocker? |
| What is the top objection? | Update objection handling if a new pattern emerges |

### Pipeline Health Indicators

| Indicator | Healthy | Warning |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Active prospects in pipeline | 15–25 | <10 |
| Calls scheduled this month | 4+ | <2 |
| Test runs in progress | 1–2 | 0 for 4+ weeks |
| Average days from first touch to call | 7–14 | >21 |
| Sequence completion rate | 80%+ | <60% (dropping prospects too early) |

---

## Escalation Rules

| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Reply rate below 5% for 2 weeks | Rewrite subject lines and opening sentences |
| No calls scheduled for 2 weeks | Expand target list or lower scoring threshold |
| 3 consecutive prospects cite same objection | Update objection handling and consider product changes |
| Prospect requests feature we cannot deliver | Log it. Do not promise. Revisit at monthly review. |
| Warm intro available | Always prioritize over cold outreach |

---

## Tools

For the design partner phase, keep it simple:

- **Target list:** `gtm/target_list_template_v1.csv` in the repo (or a Google Sheet copy)
- **Email:** Personal email (founder@domain). No automation tools yet.
- **LinkedIn:** Manual DMs from founder's personal profile.
- **Calendar:** Direct scheduling link (Calendly or Cal.com).
- **Notes:** Log in target list CSV or a simple doc after each interaction.

Do not invest in CRM or email automation until the pipeline exceeds 50 active prospects.
