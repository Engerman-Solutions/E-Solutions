# E-Solutions Design Partner Pilot

**Board-ready variance memos in hours — with reproducible numbers and an audit trail.**

---

## The problem

Your finance team spends 8–15 hours every month pulling GL data, comparing actuals to budget in spreadsheets, writing variance explanations, and formatting a memo for the board. The process is manual, error-prone, and impossible to audit three months later.

## What we deliver

E-Solutions produces your monthly board variance memo as a managed service. Each cycle:

1. **Your controller uploads** the GL export and budget file (CSV or Excel, same files you already pull from QuickBooks, Xero, or NetSuite)
2. **We validate** the data against your chart of accounts and flag any issues — nothing is analyzed until validation passes
3. **We produce** a draft variance memo: actuals vs. budget across all account categories, materiality flagging, narrative explanations for key drivers, and a KPI summary
4. **A finance QA reviewer checks** every memo for accuracy and clarity before it reaches you
5. **You review and approve** the final memo (or request revisions)
6. **We deliver** the board-ready PDF via secure portal and encrypted email

Every memo includes a checkpoint ID that ties the output to the exact source data, validation results, and review log. You can reproduce any memo at any time.

**Turnaround:** Under 48 hours from validated upload.
