# Account Targeting Rubric v1

**Purpose:** Score and rank target accounts for founder-led outreach. Use this to prioritize the shortlist.

---

## Scoring Criteria

Rate each criterion 0–3 for each prospect. Total score determines priority tier.

| # | Criterion | 0 (Poor fit) | 1 (Marginal) | 2 (Good) | 3 (Strong) |
|---|-----------|-------------|-------------|---------|-----------|
| 1 | **Stage** | Pre-seed / Late stage | Seed (no board) | Series A | Series B |
| 2 | **Employee count** | <15 or >300 | 15–25 or 250–300 | 50–150 | 25–50 or 150–250 |
| 3 | **Estimated ARR** | <$500K or >$50M | $500K–$1M | $5M–$20M | $1M–$5M or $20M–$30M |
| 4 | **Finance team size** | 0 (founder does it) or >5 | Fractional CFO only | 2–3 people | 1 Controller (sweet spot) |
| 5 | **Accounting system** | Unknown / legacy ERP | NetSuite | Xero | QuickBooks Online |
| 6 | **Board reporting frequency** | No board / annual | Quarterly | Monthly (implied) | Monthly (confirmed) |
| 7 | **Buyer accessibility** | No path | Cold outreach only | 2nd degree connection | Direct relationship or warm intro |
| 8 | **Pain signal strength** | No evidence | Industry-typical | Job posting or process hint | Explicit public pain (post, comment, job desc) |
| 9 | **Design partner potential** | Unlikely to engage | Possible but uncertain | Likely (accessible, reasonable size) | Ideal (engaged founder, feedback-willing) |
| 10 | **Implementation risk** | Multi-entity, complex COA | Some complexity | Standard SaaS P&L | Simple COA, QBO, clean data |

---

## Priority Tiers

| Score | Tier | Action |
|-------|------|--------|
| 25–30 | **A — Top priority** | Custom message, personal outreach, invest research time |
| 18–24 | **B — Strong candidates** | Semi-custom message, include in first wave |
| 12–17 | **C — Worth a touch** | Templated outreach, include in second wave |
| <12 | **D — Deprioritize** | Skip for now; revisit if pipeline is thin |

---

## How to Use This

1. List 20–30 candidate companies
2. Score each on the 10 criteria (takes ~5 min per company)
3. Sort by total score
4. Top 10–15 become the active shortlist
5. Bottom 10 go into a "watch" list
6. Re-score monthly as you learn more

---

## Scoring Shortcuts

If you do not have full information on a prospect, use these defaults:

- Unknown ARR: estimate from employee count (rough rule: $100K–$200K ARR per employee for SaaS)
- Unknown finance team: assume 1 person if <75 employees, 2–3 if 75–200
- Unknown accounting system: assume QBO if <50 employees, Xero or NetSuite if 50+
- Unknown board cadence: assume monthly if Series A+, quarterly if Seed

Company A gets personal outreach this week. Company B goes on the watch list.
