# Project Brief

## Mission

Build a trusted B2B finance workflow product and managed service that delivers auditable, board-ready monthly variance memos in hours — with reproducible numbers and a full audit trail.

## Problem being solved

Lean finance teams at Seed to Series B companies spend 8–15 hours every month manually assembling variance memos. The process involves pulling GL exports, comparing actuals to budget, identifying material variances, writing narrative explanations, and formatting a board-ready document. It is tedious, error-prone, and unrepeatable.

The pain is acute for this segment because:
- Finance teams are 1–3 people, often a solo Controller or fractional CFO
- Board reporting is mandatory and high-visibility but low-leverage for the team
- Manual spreadsheet assembly creates version control nightmares and audit risk
- Errors in board-facing numbers erode trust with investors and leadership
- Existing tools either require months of implementation (Planful, Vena) or are spreadsheet-dependent (DataRails, Cube), or solve a different problem entirely (Pilot for bookkeeping, BlackLine for close automation)
- No competitor delivers a governed, auditable variance memo workflow designed for lean startup finance teams

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

- **Company type:** Seed to Series B SaaS and services firms
- **Company size:** 20–250 employees, $1M–$30M ARR
- **Finance team:** 1–3 people (Controller, FP&A analyst, or fractional CFO)
- **Accounting system:** QuickBooks Online, Xero, or NetSuite
- **Key trait:** Producing monthly board or investor reporting packages
- **Key pain:** Spending disproportionate time on manual variance memo assembly with limited auditability

## Economic buyer

CFO or Founder (whoever owns the finance function and board reporting)

## Primary users

Controller and FP&A analyst — the people who upload data exports, review AI-drafted memos, request revisions, and approve final deliverables.

## Wedge

Monthly variance memo workflow — the single highest-frequency, highest-friction, highest-visibility deliverable that every finance team must produce and every board expects.

Why this is the right entry point:
- Universal: every company with a board produces variance memos
- Recurring: monthly cadence creates ongoing engagement and retention
- Measurable: time savings from 8–15 hours to under 1 hour is demonstrable
- High-visibility: board-facing output means the CFO/Founder sees the value directly
- Auditable by design: the workflow enforces validation, checkpoints, and approval trails — differentiators that matter to finance buyers

## Value proposition

Board-ready variance memos in hours, not days — with reproducible numbers and an audit trail.

For the Controller: replace manual spreadsheet assembly with a governed workflow. Upload your GL export and budget, get a validated draft memo with KPI summary, charts, and narrative. Review in a clean-room UI. Approve and deliver via portal or encrypted email.

For the CFO/Founder: faster close-to-board cycle, fewer fire drills, auditable numbers you can trust. The service pays for itself by saving your controller 20+ hours per month and reducing error-driven rework.

## Delivery model

Product + managed service hybrid. AI handles 80% of the work (data validation, variance analysis, narrative generation, report formatting). Human finance QA review ensures accuracy before delivery. This mirrors the model that Pilot used successfully to scale bookkeeping — AI for efficiency, humans for trust.

## Pricing hypothesis

Tiered subscription to match company growth:
- **Starter:** Up to 3 memos/month, 48h SLA, 1 data source — $3K–$4K/month
- **Pro:** Up to 2 entities, 24h SLA (or 4h for close week), multiple data sources — $8K–$10K/month
- **Enterprise:** Dedicated reviewer pool, custom retention/legal-hold, VPC options — $15K+/month

Design partners receive a discounted 90-day pilot in exchange for weekly feedback and permission to develop an anonymized case study.

## 90-day objective

Secure 2–3 design partners running live monthly variance memo workflows on real data, producing real deliverables, with documented feedback and measurable time savings.

## What success looks like before broadening scope

- At least 2 design partners have completed 2+ monthly cycles
- Measurable time savings documented (target: 60%+ reduction)
- Positive qualitative feedback from CFO/Founder on memo quality and auditability
- Clear signal on willingness to pay (verbal or written)
- Onboarding and delivery process documented well enough for a second operator to follow
- At least 1 conversion conversation from design partner to paid pilot

## What E-Solutions is not doing yet

These are explicitly deferred until the wedge is proven:
- Voice intake and conversational interfaces
- Multi-deliverable workflows (budget, forecast, cash flow)
- Client-facing chatbot or DM pairing
- Multi-agent specialization
- CRM auto-sync
- Auto-scaling infrastructure
- Broad platform features beyond the variance memo workflow
