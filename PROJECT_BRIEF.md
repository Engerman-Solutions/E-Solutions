# Project Brief

## Mission

Build a trusted B2B finance workflow product and managed service that delivers board-ready monthly variance memos faster, with auditability, reproducibility, and clear business value.

## Problem being solved

Lean finance teams at early-stage companies spend disproportionate time each month assembling variance memos manually. The process is error-prone, hard to audit, and inconsistent. Boards expect timely, clear financial narratives, but producing them is tedious grunt work that pulls controllers and FP&A away from higher-value analysis.

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

- Seed to Series B SaaS and services companies
- Annual revenue typically $1M–$30M
- Finance team of 1–3 people (Controller, FP&A analyst, or fractional CFO)
- Using QuickBooks, Xero, or NetSuite
- Producing monthly board or investor reporting packages

## Economic buyer

CFO or Founder (whoever owns the finance function)

## Primary users

Controller and FP&A analyst

## Wedge

Monthly variance memo workflow — automating the end-to-end process of pulling actuals, comparing to budget/forecast, identifying material variances, drafting narrative explanations, and producing a board-ready memo.

## Value proposition

Replace 8–15 hours of manual monthly close work with a repeatable, auditable workflow that produces a board-ready variance memo in under an hour. Finance teams get time back. Boards get better narratives. Founders get confidence in their numbers.

## 90-day objective

Secure 2–3 design partners running live monthly variance memo workflows, producing real deliverables from real data, with documented feedback loops.

## What success looks like before broadening scope

- At least 2 design partners completing a full monthly cycle
- Measurable time savings (target: 60%+ reduction in memo production time)
- Positive qualitative feedback from CFO/Founder on memo quality
- Clear signal on willingness to pay
- Documented onboarding and delivery process that a second operator could follow
