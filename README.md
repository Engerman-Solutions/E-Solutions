# E-Solutions

B2B AI finance operations for lean finance teams.

## What this is

E-Solutions builds a finance workflow product and managed service that delivers board-ready monthly variance memos faster, with auditability, reproducibility, and clear business value.

## Who we serve

- **Target customers:** Seed to Series B SaaS and services companies with lean finance teams
- **Economic buyer:** CFO or Founder
- **Main users:** Controller and FP&A

## The wedge

Monthly variance memo workflow — the single highest-friction, highest-frequency deliverable that every finance team must produce and every board expects.
